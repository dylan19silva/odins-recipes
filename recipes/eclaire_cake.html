Eclaire Cake

If you ever get stressed about cooking or don't feel like a confident cook, worry not! This recipe is super easy, cheap, and quick for any skill level!

Just grab these few things below and in no time people will be thinking you are a master pastry chef.

  • 1 Box of graham cracker
  • 16 oz of cool whip
  • 2 boxes of french vanilla pudding
  • 1 container of fudge spread icing
  • 4 cups of milk

Directions

  1. Follow instructions on pudding box to create pudding in a bowl
  2. Once mixed well, add cool whip bit by bit
  3. Line bottom of dish with graham cracker, pour 1/2 of the pudding mixture over the graham crackers.
  4. Create another layer of graham crackers, pour rest of pudding over.
  5. Add one final layer of graham cracker over top. Spread icing over top, let chill for 5-8 hours. Enjoy!
